Our Favorites

First time visiting Boston? Want to pretend to be Chris and Emmy for a day? Here are a few of our favorite activities. We’ll happily join you during any of these for a bite, a beer or a chat.

  • Get coffee at Jaho, South End Buttery, Thinking Cup or Flour – or all of the above, multiple times throughout your stay
  • Take a stroll down Newbury Street and then have a picnic on the esplanade
  • Take pictures with the Make Way for Ducklings statue in Boston Common
  • Try the cheese samples at South End Formaggio
  • Go for a run on Charles River
  • Get the burger at Drink (it’s so good it’s not even on the menu)
  • Go kayaking on the Charles River
  • Watch some whales
  • Eat Chris’s favorite pizza at Picco (pepperoni, add salami)
  • Drink Chris’s favorite beers at Lord Hobo
  • Ride a Hubway bike around Boston (Chris says – WEAR A HELMET! – we have extras.)
  • Eat everything at Island Creek Oyster Bar – especially shrimp cocktail, for Emmy
  • Enjoy some cool art at the ICA
